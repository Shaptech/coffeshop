About JBS

At JBS, we're dedicated to crafting exceptional baked goods that delight the senses and bring people together. Our story began with a simple yet profound passion for baking, and over the years, we've evolved into a renowned artisanal bakery that's committed to excellence in every aspect.

Our Mission:

To Make Every Day a Little Sweeter We believe that baked goods have the power to transcend mere sustenance and become an integral part of our daily lives. That's why we're on a mission to make every day a little sweeter, one delicious treat at a time. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, indulging in a guilty pleasure, or simply seeking comfort in a warm, freshly baked loaf, we're here to provide you with an unparalleled baking experience.

Our Values:

Quality, Integrity, and Community At JBS, we're guided by a set of core values that inform every decision we make:

Quality:
We're uncompromising in our commitment to using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to craft baked goods that are truly exceptional.
Integrity:
We operate with transparency, honesty, and integrity in all our interactions, whether with customers, suppliers, or colleagues.
Community:
We believe that baking has the power to bring people together, and we're dedicated to building strong relationships with our customers, partners, and the wider community.

Our Story:

A Legacy of Love and Baking Our bakery was founded by Precious, a passionate baker with a lifelong love affair with the art of baking. Growing up in a family of avid bakers, Precious learned the secrets of traditional baking from a young age and went on to hone their skills in some of the world's top bakeries.

With a clear vision and a relentless drive to innovate, Precious established JBS with the goal of creating a world-class bakery that would become a beloved institution in the community. Today, we're proud to be a part of this vibrant community, serving up freshly baked goods that bring joy to our customers.

Our Team:

The Heart and Soul of Our Bakery At JBS, we're fortunate to have a talented and dedicated team of bakers, decorators, and customer service professionals who share our passion for baking and commitment to excellence. From our experienced head bakers to our friendly front-of-house staff, every member of our team plays a vital role in delivering an exceptional customer experience.

Bakery Trivia.

What types of baked goods do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of baked goods, including cakes, cupcakes, cookies, brownies, Muffins, scones, and bread.
